# 📊 Business Model Canvas – **Data Shield**

| **Block** | **Details** |
|---|---|
| **Value Proposition** | • **Proactive data protection** – real‑time alerts before any deletion or modification.<br>• **Full transparency** – a searchable audit trail that shows *what*, *when* and *why* data changed.<br>• **User‑centric control** – self‑service portal for users to approve, postpone or archive at‑risk data.<br>• **Regulatory compliance** – built‑in GDPR, CCPA, HIPAA audit logs and exportable reports.<br>• **Peace of mind** – reduces risk of accidental loss, legal penalties, and reputational damage. |
| **Customer Segments** | 1. **Enterprise SaaS platforms** (CRM, ERP, HRIS) that store critical user data.<br>2. **FinTech & HealthTech** firms with strict compliance mandates.<br>3. **Mid‑size B2B SaaS** looking for a plug‑and‑play compliance add‑on.<br>4. **Data‑privacy‑focused consumers** (via white‑label integrations). |
| **Channels** | • **Direct sales** – enterprise account executives (Axentx BD team).<br>• **Marketplace integrations** – AWS Marketplace, Azure Marketplace, GCP Marketplace.<br>• **Partner ecosystem** – embedded in Axentx’s existing products (e.g., Iceoryx2 IPC library) and third‑party SaaS platforms.<br>• **Developer community** – open‑source core on GitHub, documentation site, webinars.<br>• **Content & SEO** – whitepapers on compliance, case studies, blog series. |
| **Revenue Streams** | 1. **Subscription SaaS** – tiered plans (Starter, Professional, Enterprise) billed monthly/annually.<br>2. **Usage‑based add‑on** – per‑million‑events monitoring fee for high‑volume customers.<br>3. **Professional services** – implementation, custom policy engine, compliance audit assistance.<br>4. **OEM licensing** – white‑label SDK for partners to embed Data Shield in their products.<br>5. **Support contracts** – SLA‑based premium support for mission‑critical deployments. |
| **Cost Structure** | • **R&D** – salaries for senior engineers, product manager, compliance specialist.<br>• **Infrastructure** – cloud compute (event streaming, storage, audit‑log DB), CDN for portal UI.<br>• **Security & compliance** – third‑party audits, certifications (ISO‑27001, SOC‑2).<br>• **Sales & Marketing** – channel partner commissions, content production, conference sponsorships.<br>• **Open‑source maintenance** – CI/CD pipelines, community management, documentation.<br>• **Legal & licensing** – MIT‑licensed core, proprietary compliance modules. |
| **Key Resources** | • **Core codebase** – open‑source “business” layer (monitoring, notification, audit log).<br>• **Axentx AI‑workforce** – automated PRD generation, code scaffolding (vLLM, SGLang) to accelerate feature delivery.<br>• **Data assets** – existing 8.8 M auto‑pairs & compliance‑related corpora for training anomaly‑detection models.<br>• **Compliance expertise** – internal legal counsel, external auditors.<br>• **Partner network** – Iceoryx2 (IPC) for low‑latency event capture, cloud providers for managed services. |
| **Key Activities** | 1. **Product development** – build monitoring agents, notification engine, transparency portal.<br>2. **Compliance engineering** – embed GDPR/CCPA/HIPAA rule sets, generate audit‑ready logs.<br>3. **Integration SDKs** – language bindings (Python, Go, Java) and webhook APIs.<br>4. **Continuous security testing** – automated penetration tests, static analysis, bug‑bounty program.<br>5. **Customer success** – onboarding, policy configuration workshops, quarterly compliance reviews.<br>6. **Marketplace publishing** – packaging for AWS/Azure/GCP marketplaces. |
| **Key Partners** | • **Cloud providers** – AWS, Azure, GCP (for managed event streaming & storage).<br>• **Compliance auditors** – third‑party firms that certify Data Shield for industry standards.<br>• **Axentx product portfolio** – Iceoryx2 (fast IPC), Surrogate‑1‑Harvest (data pipelines) for seamless data flow.<br>• **Open‑source communities** – vLLM & SGLang for scalable inference when adding AI‑driven anomaly detection.<br>• **Channel partners** – system integrators, VARs that sell SaaS bundles to regulated industries. |
| **Unique Advantages (Why We Win)** | • **Axentx AI‑driven pipeline** – rapid feature iteration and validation without heavy manual effort.<br>• **Leverage existing data‑pair assets** to train predictive models that flag risky deletions before they happen.<br>• **Modular open‑source core** reduces lock‑in, encourages community contributions, accelerates adoption.<br>• **Cross‑product synergy** – Data Shield can be bundled with Iceoryx2 for high‑throughput IPC environments where data loss is catastrophic. |
